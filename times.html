Rates

  • Rates are for cats from the same household sharing.
  • All meals are included in the daily boarding rate.
  • Because of the intensive regime of cleaning, disinfection and preparation required before and after each stay, our daily rate is charged for the day of delivery and the day of collection.
  • Balance of accounts must be settled at the time of collection and payment by cash, cheque or bank transfer is accepted.
  • Owners are responsible for any veterinary bills that are incurred during their cat's stay and must be settled on collection. Please advise us if your cat is insured.
