Welcome to Garden Cattery


Our cat, Jerry

Garden Cattery, situated 13 miles south of Norwich, is based in and serves Bunwell and the surrounding areas such as Norwich, Attleborough, Wymondham and Long Stratton, although we also have guests from much further afield. Garden Cattery is home to myself (Susan) and my husband Shane, along with our own cat, Jerry. We are lifelong cat lovers and your cat(s) will be looked after as though they were our own whilst they are in our care.

The cattery is situated in our back garden so it is perfect for us to be able to keep an eye on all our boarders whilst they play, snooze or just watch the world go by. We are a completely dog-free environment. The pens are in a courtyard setting with bird feeders and a water feature to keep the cats amused and interested during their stay.

We have a 5 star license rating from South Norfolk Council (this is our license) under the Animal Welfare Regulations 2018 (licence number AB69 valid from 1/1/2019 until 31/12/2021). We also have over 50 reviews on our Facebook page, all of which have rated us 5 out of 5.

The comfort and wellbeing of our boarding cats is paramount to us and to that end the cattery has been built to the International Cat Care (ICC) guidelines, previously Feline Advisory Bureau (FAB), guidelines.

We know all cats are different, react differently to being boarded and have their own unique characters, which is why we like to get to know them (on their own terms of course!) so that they get the individual attention they need whilst away from their normal environment.
